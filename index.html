Newsletter with Recommender

Thousands of papers are published daily. Let ArticleLens help you focus on what matters most.

  • Select Preferred Categories: Choose from various Arxiv categories that align with your research interests.
  • Input Topics or Queries: Specify topics like "Prompt Injection" to narrow down the papers.
  • Customize Summaries: Add or remove sections such as "Disadvantages" to tailor the information you receive.
  • Daily Updates: Receive summaries every day, keeping you updated on the latest developments.

Research Guide

Get a structured reading path based on your current interests or articles.

  • Provide a Topic or Article: Input a specific article you're reading or a topic you're interested in.
  • Access Ranked References: The system extracts and ranks references based on publication date, citation count, and complexity level.
  • Suggested Reading Order: Follow a path from introductory-level papers to advanced research for comprehensive understanding.
  • Enhance Your Research: Efficiently build knowledge and stay informed about foundational and cutting-edge studies.
